About Foodies!

Founded in 2020 by four entrepreneurs, Foodies is a start-up company operating in the United Kingdom. Headquartered in Glasgow, we employ a team of 4 talented employees.

Foodies aims to be a world leader in online and mobile food ordering, providing customers with an easy way to order food from our individual partners. We use our technology to make food discovery exciting and make home-cooked meals available to everyone. We aim to bring together thousands of customers with thousands of individual cookers, serving a diverse range of cuisine types, thus creating the world's greatest food community.

It is all about offering home-made food with friendly prices.
